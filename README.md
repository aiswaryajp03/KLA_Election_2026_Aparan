# KLA_Election_2026_Aparan

## 🔍 Major Insights

- **52 constituencies affected** — ~37% of Kerala’s 140 constituencies  
- **144 candidates involved**, across **62 clashing first names**

## Key Observations

- **Kozhikode (10/13)** and **Kannur (8/11)** emerge as major hotspots  
- **Pathanamthitta** stands out with **zero clashes**
- Nearly **60% of clash candidates are Independents**
- Most clashes are **cross-alliance**, often between a **party candidate and an Independent**

##  High-Intensity Constituencies

- **Koyilandy** and **Aruvikkara** see **over 70%** of candidates involved in clashes

##  Notable Name Clustering

Multiple candidates share the same first name in:
- Azhikode  
- Beypore  
- Koduvally  
- Kothamangalam  
- Kozhikode North  
- Peravoor
